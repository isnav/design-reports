# Design Reports

A personal dumping ground of compiled research on design — covering topics such as:

- Design processes and workflows
- Playbooks and frameworks
- Measuring design value and impact
- Team structures and ways of working
- … and so on

## A note on sources

Most of this is compiled with the help of AI tools. While the goal is to surface real sources and highlight useful patterns rather than generate opinions, **treat everything here as a starting point, not a source of truth.** Verify anything you intend to use or rely on.

## Contributing

This is a personal reference, but if you spot something worth adding or a source worth correcting, feel free to open an issue or PR.
